Key Correlations Across Reports

CONVERGENCE 7/7 firms
AI as Strategic Imperative
All firms agree: AI adoption is non-negotiable for 2026 competitiveness. But definitions vary widely from "agentic AI" (McKinsey, Gartner) to "AI for productivity" (KPMG, PwC).
CONVERGENCE 5/7 firms
Workforce Disruption Inevitable
McKinsey (robotics), KPMG (35% reductions), PwC (AI generalist), EY (hybrid era), Gartner (tiny teams) all predict fundamental workforce changes.
DIVERGENCE Notable gap
Tech vs. Macro Lens
McKinsey/Gartner focus on technology capabilities. BCG/Accenture focus on geopolitical/economic context. Few integrate both perspectives.
DIVERGENCE Critical gap
Governance Afterthought
AI governance treated as implementation detail, not strategic pillar. Only PwC dedicates prediction to "Responsible AI". PALO principles largely absent.
