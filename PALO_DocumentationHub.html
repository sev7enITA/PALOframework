Documentation map

Read the PALO lifecycle

PALO connects governance decisions across the lifecycle. Each phase should leave a proportionate record that can be reviewed, updated, and handed to the next responsible person.

01 Ideation

Define the purpose

Describe the problem, intended benefit, affected people, decision authority, and the reasons AI is being considered.

02 Assessment

Classify the case

Use context, purpose, actor, impact, and delegated authority to route the system to the right level of review.

03 Design

Choose controls

Turn rights, safety, security, data, transparency, and human-agency risks into design constraints and review gates.

04 Development

Build with evidence

Record functional intent, development environment, tests, changes, approvals, and the assumptions made by the team.

05 Deployment

Release with gates

Set owners, monitoring, KPIs/KRIs, user information, incident routes, and a clear decision to deploy or pause.

06 Review and retirement

Close the loop

Review performance and incidents, preserve the final evidence, manage change, and retire the system responsibly.

PALO ecosystem

PolicyWatcher: deployment and monitoring evidence

PolicyWatcher is a separate civic-tech portal by Fabrizio Degni that monitors public privacy policies and terms of service, maps policy changes, and publishes methodology and source-quality context. It complements PALO after deployment, where provider policy changes and external signals become part of ongoing monitoring.

Related project

Observe policy change outside the system

Use the portal as an external watch surface for public policy changes, evidence records, regional context, and policy signals. It is not a PALO module, legal certification, or replacement for official sources.

Open PolicyWatcher

Traceability

Read the evidence and limits

PolicyWatcher exposes its Observatory, timeline, confidence methodology, and Trust & Quality area so readers can distinguish public evidence, dataset QA, AI-assisted analysis, and open questions.

ObservatoryMethodologyTrust & Quality
Integration boundary. PolicyWatcher can inform a monitoring or review cycle, but the PALO evidence record should preserve the original source, review date, decision owner, and applicable official requirement. AI-assisted summaries remain subject to human verification.
