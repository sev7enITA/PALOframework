🎯 WHAT THIS APP DOES

P.A.L.O. Framework Toolbox is a privacy-first, offline PRE-SCREENING tool for preliminary self-assessment of AI governance posture, based entirely on the public regulatory sources listed above.

• Helps understand your AI governance readiness
• Provides educational guidance on EU AI Act compliance areas
• Generates pre-screening reports for internal review
• Supports local evidence organization for audit-readiness
• Completely offline — all data stays on your device
• No internet required — no data ever leaves your device

TOOLS INCLUDED:
✓ FRIA Assessment — Fundamental Rights Impact Assessment wizard (based on EU AI Act requirements)
✓ Risk Tiering Calculator — Classify AI systems by EU AI Act risk category (Art. 6, Annex III)
✓ KPI Generator — AI governance KPIs aligned with PALO framework dimensions
✓ AI Model Canvas — Visual planning canvas for responsible AI deployment
✓ Framework Comparison — Compare governance approaches across frameworks
✓ Human Agency Risk Map — 18 delegated activities observatory with mitigation guidance
✓ 2026 Tech Trends Observatory — Analysis of 7 consulting firms' predictions vs. PALO governance gaps
✓ AuditBench Explorer — Alignment auditing simulator for 14 hidden AI behavior categories
